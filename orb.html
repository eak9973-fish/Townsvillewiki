The Emerald Orb

The Emerald Orb is a sentient construct of pure energy believed to be the foundation of reality itself. it is the prime force responsible for creation, balance, and the continued stability of existence within the known world.

Nature and Purpose

The Emerald Orb is not merely an object, but a conscious entity capable of thought and intent. Without its influence, reality is believed to fracture or collapse entirely.

Creation of the First Beings

the Emerald Orb began creation by forming powerful entities to oversee and protect existence. The first of these beings was Osmious, who would later become one of the leaders of The Spire.

Following Osmious, the Orb created Tro and Raygon, both of whom also rose to leadership within The Spire.

The final leader created by the Orb was The Cook, whose purpose remains a subject of debate among historians and lorekeepers.

Creation of the World

After the creation of these beings, the Emerald Orb shaped the world as it is known today. Continents, life, and the underlying laws of reality were formed through its influence. Even now, many believe the Orb continues to subtly guide events to preserve balance.
