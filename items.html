Items

Items are powerful artifacts, tools, and constructs that have played significant roles in the history of Townsville. Some are widely known and documented, while others exist only in sealed records or whispered legends.

A.E.G.I.S

A.E.G.I.S, commonly referred to by villagers as "The Protector of the Skies", is a massive satellite-mounted orbital cannon stationed high above the planet.

The structure measures approximately 3 miles in width and 6 miles in height, making it one of the largest artificial constructs ever created. A.E.G.I.S is capable of firing concentrated energy beams of catastrophic power—some theoretical projections suggest enough force to annihilate small celestial bodies.

Despite its immense destructive potential, A.E.G.I.S has only been activated once in recorded history. Details surrounding this event remain classified.
