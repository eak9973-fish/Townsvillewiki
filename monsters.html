Characters and Creatures

This page serves as an index of all known creatures, entities, and notable individuals documented within the world of Townsville. Entries range from commonly encountered wildlife to rare or legendary beings, as well as historically significant figures.

Overview

The inhabitants of Townsville include a wide variety of lifeforms, many of which possess unusual biological traits or supernatural abilities. While some creatures coexist peacefully with local populations, others pose significant threats and are carefully monitored by researchers and authorities.

Known Creatures

  • Slimes – Amorphous organisms with abilities tied to coloration
